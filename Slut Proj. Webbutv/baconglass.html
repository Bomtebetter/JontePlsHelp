Bacon Ice cream

Ingredients: Amount:
Ice Cream base
Cream 5 dl
Dulce de leche 1 jar
Maple syrup 2 tbsp
Maplebacon
Bacon 1 pkt
Maple syrup 1 tbsp

Bacon ice cream, a funny test which turned out to be great!

The idea behind bacon ice cream came when my dad had purchased an ice cream maker for fun. He wrote a text to the family chat "Hej, jag har köpt en glassmaskin vad vill ni göra för glass?" Which means "Hey, I just purchased an icecream maker, what icecream do you want to make?" I answered with one word, bacon because everything is better with bacon. He just laughed but he went with it. So he purchased a lot of bacon, dulce de leche, and maple syrup. We began experimenting with different amounts of bacon, dulce de leche and maple syrup to get the right mix. After 3 batches we found the right mix. It was perfect. In my opinion this is a better version of the salted caramel ice cream.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 150 degrees.
  2. Brush the bacon with maple syrup on both sides.
  3. Cook the bacon in the oven until its crisp with your desired color.
  4. Mix your cream, dulce de leche, and maple syrup. Add the bacon in as ripple. (Save some for topping.)
  5. Put your mix in the ice cream maker and just wait it out. (if you don't own an ice cream maker just skip this one.)
  6. If you don't own an ice cream maker just go and stir it a couple of times while it is in the freezer.
  7. Enjoy!
