Why I made this site

I made this site because I simply love food. Just the ability to experience the world through taste. Being able to mix and match all the various wonderful flavors.

I also wanted to show off a bit of my food journey and maybe inspire you, the reader to begin a fun food journey with mixing and matching from all the different flavors like salt, sweet, bitter, sour, and umami. The video describes how I feel when I succeed with a dish.
